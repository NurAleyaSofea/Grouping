About Us

SwanHeritage is a partnership form of business that is being registered in 1st January 2019 and being established in 1st November 2021.
It was developed to introduce the business of hijab and scarf boutiques that meets the needs of women of all kinds especially to the Muslim women in Malaysia.
SwanHeritage business consist of manufactured product of premium quality scarf that are made from several type of fabric materials Chiffon, Cotton, and Crinkled Cotton Mesh Fabric.
There are several types of scarfs that is available to choose from based on the preference of the customer, for example Nayla Square, Medina Arabic Shawl, Leesa Chiffon Shawl, Alma Series, Hover Shawl Cotton Printed.
The SwanHeritage physical store is located in Bnadar Baru Bangi, Selangor. It is also being operated online on several social media platform such as WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, twitter and Telegram. Our business hours for physical store are being operated from Monday to Saturday and being closed every Sunday and public holiday.




Mission

• Provide customers with a simple and quality product.
• To provide a unique veil to meet the needs of every woman as each individual has their own personality.
• To maintain the highest standards in terms of customer service, product quality, and a wide range of options to meet the needs of the target market. This also helps maintain the image and reputation of the organization in the Malaysian market.


Vision

• To be one of Malaysia’s leading brands in exclusive scarves, offering high quality, stylish and elegant creations, targeting customer profiles primarily from Malaysia, as well as globally through a strong online presence for women.
• We operate perfectly in every aspect, value beauty and style in the fashion industry, and we aim to maintain our brand name and reputation.



Objective

• Become the leading scarf boutique for classy and stylish women in Malaysia by offering the latest fashion and the highest level of customer attention.
• To provide hijab boutiques with a comfortable and easily accessible environment especially by offering online purchases.
• Retain regular customers and also find new ones.
• Create and produce products that are easy to use and traditional or modern at any age.
